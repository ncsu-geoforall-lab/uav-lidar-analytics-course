Assignment reports

For each topic (as indicated in the schedule) you will submit a brief paper with the following sections:

  • Introduction: task, problem, background
  • Approach: general description of methods and concepts
  • Results: present the results, including maps, graphs, tables with comments
  • Discussion: was there anything unexpected or problematic in the results? Were there any issues related to the methdology? Also discuss the software issues, including fixes and suggestions for improvements here.
  • Conclusion: 1-2 sentences what you have learned
  • References, if applicable
Note that the submitted report should be in PDF, use naming convention LastnameFirstname_HW#.pdf.
