3D reconstruction from multiple photos

Outline:

  • concept of 3D reconstruction from overlapping imagery
  • properties of imagery set and their influence on the resulting model
  • setting up a laboratory experiment
  • software for 3D reconstruction

Lecture:

  • To be added, for now see SFMedu: A Structure from Motion System for Education by Jianxiong Xiao, Princeton Vision & Robotics
Supplemental materials:
  • ISPRS 2016 paper on Reconstruction of Palmyra from tourist photos
  • ISPRS 2016 paper on Reconstruction of popular monuments from crowdsourced photos

Assignment

Perform laboratory experiment to investigate the impact of the number and orientation of photos, shape and features of a landscape model and light on 3D reconstruction of a physical landscape model from multiple overlapping imagery.
  • Lab Experiment

Homework:

Prepare short report on 3D reconstruction from series of photographs and parameters that influence the model accuracy and distortions.
